The Avengers Crystal Collectors

Thanos gives you a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

Defeat Thanos by matching your total score to the random number. Thanos wins if you go over.

The crystals are assigned new values for each new game.
